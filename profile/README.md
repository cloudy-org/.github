## ☁️ What we do? *(or what will we do...)*
Our goal is to create open-source software that does **exactly what it is supposed to do**, with emphasis on configuration and choice. 

Software at cloudy-org is free as in freedom, transparent and efficient; software at cloudy-org follows our **[Philosophy][philosophy_link]** which manifests as that.

**Our Philosophy: https://cloudy-org.github.io/wiki/philosophy**

Regardless we're just a group of dedicated devs who love developing 🦅 **FOSS**. 

We build 🐧 Linux first but you'll find many of our applications with cross-platform support.

## 🛠️ Contributing
We're open to contributions, after all that's what makes open-source software... **open-source software**...

Although we have contribution guidelines and some conventions we recommend you follow, otherwise your contribution could be discarded.

## ❓ Why did I create this organisation?
*If the previous sections and philosophy above was not enough...*

This organisation is a place I and others (who share frustrations in bloated, inefficient and anti-consumer software) build tools for ourselves that are **fast**, focused on their **intended purpose** and are **respectful** to you and your computer. If the tools are helpful to us, someone else may find it helpful too.
